2. The Pre-Flight Somatic Matrix

6-Point Checklist (Before Every Recording Session)

  1. Breath: 5 deep breaths. Stomach full. Hold 4 counts. Release slow.
  2. Posture: Spine tall. Shoulders back. Chin level. No tension in the neck.
  3. Jaw Release: Gently massage the masseter. Open and close slowly.
  4. Throat Softness: Hum. Feel the vibration in your chest and sinuses.
  5. Hydration Check: Drink 8 oz of water. Wait 2 minutes.
  6. Sobriety Acknowledgment: State your sobriety day out loud. "This is Day X."

Total time: 5–7 minutes. Non-negotiable before every vocal take.

3. The LUFS Ladder

5-Step Mixing Reference

Goal: -7 LUFS. Journey:

  1. -10/-11 dB Goal Setting: Rough mix. All tracks at appropriate levels.
  2. -9 dB Saturation Stage 1: First saturation pass. Glue the mix.
  3. -8 dB Fill Stage: Stereo width, ambience, effects. Create depth.
  4. Saturation Stages 2–3: Intentional distortion layers.
  5. -7 dB Final Limiter + Export: Protect peaks. True Peak -0.3dB.

4. The S3 Check-in Protocol

5 Steps Before Studio Sessions

  1. Close Eyes (10 sec): Remove visual stimulus. Turn inward.
  2. S1 — Body Sensation (20 sec): Scan from head to feet. Describe in one word.
  3. S2 — Mental Load (20 sec): What's on your mind? Name it without judgment.
  4. S3 — Metacognition (20 sec): The part that observes all of this. Just notice it exists.
  5. Intention Statement (10 sec): Out loud. Anchors the work.

Total time: 80–90 seconds.
