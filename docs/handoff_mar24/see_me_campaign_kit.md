**One line:** Ethan Payton makes the music Drake fans find at 1AM when the playlist ends and the algorithm starts thinking for itself.

**Never say:**
- ❌ "The next Drake / 6LACK / Bryson Tiller"
- ❌ "If you like [bigger artist], you'll love this"
- ❌ "NOT ready for this"
- ❌ Any variation of "the algorithm says"
- ❌ Fire emojis as a substitute for confidence

**Always convey:**
- ✅ The listener discovered something — they weren't sold it
- ✅ Mood over metrics
- ✅ The music speaks before the copy does
- ✅ Independent is a flex, not an apology
