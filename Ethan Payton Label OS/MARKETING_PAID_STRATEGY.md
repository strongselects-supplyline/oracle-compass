## 1. CORE PRINCIPLE

**Paid promotion is FUEL, not FIRE.**

You never pay to promote content that isn't already performing organically. Paid amplifies winners — it doesn't rescue losers. The organic performance (from Depts 2 + 3) identifies what resonates. Paid scales it.

**The Decision Chain:**
```
Organic content posted → 48 hours → Check sends-per-reach + watch-through
    → IF above threshold: promote it
    → IF below threshold: iterate on next piece of content (don't spend money)
```

**Budget Reality:** You are on a DoorDash income bridge ($1,800/month). Marketing budget must be surgical — every dollar is measured against cost-per-stream and cost-per-follower. No vanity campaigns. No "brand awareness" at this stage. Everything is conversion-oriented.

## 3. THE THREE PAID CHANNELS

### Channel 1: Meta Ads (Instagram/Facebook → Spotify)

**What it is:** Paid promotion of your content (reels, images) on Instagram and Facebook that drives users to a smart link → Spotify.

**2026 Benchmarks:**
- Cost per click (to smart link): $0.15–$0.50
- Cost per stream (effective): $0.03–$0.08 (Tier 1 markets)
- Click-to-stream conversion: 35–45% with proper funnel
- Minimum test budget: $50

**The Funnel (critical — NEVER link directly to Spotify from an ad):**

```
IG Reel Ad (your best-performing organic reel)
    → Smart Link (feature.fm or similar, with Meta Pixel installed)
        → Spotify (stream/save/follow)
```

**Why the smart link matters:** The Meta Pixel on the smart link page tracks conversions. Without it, you're spending blind. With it, Meta optimizes for people most likely to actually stream — not just click.

**What to promote (always your best organic content):**
- The reel with highest sends-per-reach from the past 2 weeks
- The Talk to 'Em video that got the most profile visits
- NEVER promote a static image or a low-performing reel

**Targeting:**

| Audience Type | Who | When to Use |
|--------------|-----|-------------|
| **Lookalike (1%)** | People who look like your current Spotify listeners | Best for new releases — finds new potential fans |
| **Interest-based** | Brent Faiyaz, Bryson Tiller, PARTYNEXTDOOR, The Weeknd fans + R&B interest | Broader reach, lower conversion, good for awareness |
| **Retargeting** | People who clicked your smart link but didn't stream | Cheapest conversions — they already showed interest |
| **Custom (engaged)** | People who engaged with your IG content in last 30 days | Convert warm audience to Spotify followers |

**Geographic targeting:**
- Primary: Chicago, Denver, Minneapolis, New York, Phoenix, Dallas (your top S4A cities)
- Secondary: Milwaukee, Toronto, Calgary, Seattle (Midwest/Mountain/Canadian corridor)
- Avoid: Global spray. Your audience is concentrated. Target where they already are.

**Age/Gender:** 25–34 male (60% of your demo). Don't exclude female — just weight male higher.

### Channel 2: Spotify Campaign Kit

Spotify's native promotion tools. Three weapons:

#### Marquee (Full-screen pop-up on Spotify Home)

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Cost | $0.30–$0.70 per click |
| Minimum spend | $100 |
| Eligibility | 1,000+ streams in past 28 days in target market |
| Best for | New releases in first 2 weeks |
| Target | "Previously engaged" audience (people who've heard you before) |
| Expected result | 2–5x increase in release-week streams for that segment |

**When to use Marquee:**
- EP release week (May 23+)
- Only if Day 3 stream velocity suggests the track is gaining traction
- Target: "Previously Engaged" audience (cheapest, highest conversion)
- Budget: $100 per release, maximum

#### Showcase (Sponsored recommendation on Home feed)

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Cost | $0.15–$0.50 per click |
| Minimum spend | $100–250 |
| Eligibility | 1,000+ streams in past 28 days |
| Best for | Older catalog tracks that are gaining momentum |
| Target | New listeners most likely to stream based on listening patterns |

**When to use Showcase:**
- When a track shows unexpected organic growth (viral TikTok moment, playlist add)
- To push a waterfall single that's performing above baseline
- NOT during release week (Marquee is better for that)

#### Discovery Mode (Royalty trade for algorithmic boost)

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Cost | 30% royalty reduction on promoted track |
| Upfront cost | $0 |
| Where it works | Radio, Autoplay, algorithmic playlists |
| Expected result | +50% saves, +44% playlist adds in first month |
| Eligibility | Any released track |

**When to activate Discovery Mode:**
- On tracks with 7-day save rate ≥ 3% (proves the track converts when heard)
- On tracks where you want to accelerate algorithmic placement
- NOT on tracks with low save rates (amplifying a track people don't save = wasted royalties)
- Ideal combo: Discovery Mode + Marquee on same track = 2x monthly active listener growth

**Standing Discovery Mode decisions:**

| Track | Activate? | Reasoning |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Hollywood Fever | YES | Already your algorithmic engine. 84% programmed. Feed it. |
| SEE ME | MAYBE | 6.55% save rate = excellent conversion. Test for 30 days. |
| EP Focus Track (best performer) | YES (after Day 14 data) | Only the winner gets Discovery Mode. |
| Vault singles | Per-track decision | Activate on any that hit 3%+ save rate in first 7 days |

### Channel 3: TikTok Promote (minimal, test only)

TikTok has a native "Promote" button that boosts existing videos. Very cheap ($5–20 per boost) but conversion to Spotify is unclear for music artists. Use ONLY as a $10 test when a video is already performing above average organically. Do not build a strategy around this.

## 4. THE META ADS PLAYBOOK (Step-by-Step)

### Setup (one-time, 30 min)

1. Create Meta Business Suite account (business.facebook.com)
2. Create Ad Account linked to your IG
3. Install Meta Pixel on your smart link page (feature.fm supports this natively)
4. Create Custom Audiences:
   - IG Engagers (last 30 days)
   - Smart Link Visitors (from Pixel)
   - Lookalike (1%) based on IG Engagers
5. Set up Conversion Event: "Link Click" or "Page View" on smart link

### Campaign Creation Template

**Campaign Objective:** Traffic (optimized for Link Clicks)
**Budget:** $5–10/day for 5–7 days per test ($25–70 total)
**Placement:** Instagram Reels + Instagram Feed (exclude Facebook unless budget allows testing)
**Creative:** Your best-performing organic reel (download without watermark, upload as ad creative)
**CTA:** "Listen Now" or "Stream Free"
**Link:** Smart link (NOT direct Spotify link)
**Audience:** Lookalike 1% OR Interest-based (test both with $25 each, kill the loser after 3 days)

### The $50 Test Protocol

For every release, you get ONE $50 test:

| Day | Action | Budget Spent |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Day 1–2 | Post content organically. Wait for performance data. | $0 |
| Day 3 | Check sends-per-reach. If ≥ 2%: proceed. If < 2%: no ad spend this release. | $0 |
| Day 3 | Create 2 ad sets: Lookalike 1% ($25) + Interest-based ($25). Both use same reel. | $0 (ads in review) |
| Day 4–6 | Both ads run at $5/day each. Monitor CTR and CPC. | $30 |
| Day 7 | Kill the underperformer. Double budget on winner for 3 more days. | $20 |
| Day 10 | Campaign ends. Assess: streams gained, cost per stream, followers gained. | Total: $50 |

**If cost-per-stream exceeds $0.10:** Kill immediately. The content or targeting isn't working.
**If cost-per-stream is $0.03–$0.07:** Winner. Consider extending budget by $25–50.

## 5. TRIGGER CRITERIA (When to Spend Money)

This is the most important section. **Never spend unless at least ONE trigger fires.**

### Meta Ads Triggers

| Trigger | Metric | Threshold | Action |
|---------|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Organic reel overperforms** | Sends-per-reach | ≥ 3% (1.5x your average) | Promote that specific reel with $50 test |
| **Profile visits spike** | IG Insights | 2x weekly average | Promote your best recent reel to capture the momentum |
| **Release week** | Calendar | Upload +3 days (Day 3 of sprint) | Run $50 test on best snippet/Talk to 'Em |
| **TikTok video pops** | TikTok views | 10K+ views (5x your average) | Promote similar content on IG (don't promote on TikTok — use IG to convert) |

### Spotify Campaign Kit Triggers

| Trigger | Metric | Threshold | Action |
|---------|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Strong release-week start** | Day 3 stream velocity | 100+ streams/day | Activate Marquee ($100) targeting Previously Engaged |
| **Save rate confirms quality** | 7-day save rate | ≥ 3% | Activate Discovery Mode on that track |
| **Unexpected catalog growth** | Weekly stream increase | Track gains 50%+ week-over-week | Activate Showcase ($100) on that track |
| **Playlist add momentum** | Editorial or algorithmic playlist add | Any playlist with 1K+ followers | Discovery Mode immediately (capitalize on placement) |
| **Combined signal** | Save rate ≥ 3% AND stream velocity 100+/day | Both thresholds met simultaneously | Marquee + Discovery Mode together (2x growth) |

### NEVER SPEND Conditions

| Condition | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Save rate below 2% after 7 days | Track isn't converting. Paid won't fix a product problem. |
| No organic content performing above 2% sends-per-reach | You don't have a winner to amplify. Make better content first. |
| Monthly DoorDash income is below target | Income stability > marketing spend. Never sacrifice the floor. |
| Emotional impulse ("I just feel like this one deserves a push") | Feelings are not data. Wait for metrics to confirm. |
| Copying what another artist did | Their audience, budget, and catalog are different. Your triggers are YOUR data. |

## 7. MEASUREMENT & ROI FRAMEWORK

### Per-Campaign Metrics

After every paid campaign, log these numbers:

| Metric | Target | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Cost per stream | $0.03–$0.08 | Above $0.10 = kill campaign |
| Cost per smart link click | $0.15–$0.35 | Above $0.50 = creative/targeting problem |
| Click-to-stream conversion | 35%+ | Below 25% = smart link friction (too many steps) |
| Cost per new follower (Spotify) | $0.50–$1.50 | Above $2.00 = targeting wrong audience |
| 30-day listener retention | 20%+ still streaming after 30 days | Below 10% = attracted wrong audience |

### The ROI Question

At $0.004–$0.005 per stream royalty rate (typical for Amuse/independent), a $50 campaign that generates 1,000 streams earns back ~$5 in royalties. That's -90% ROI on direct streaming revenue.

**So why spend at all?**

Because the ROI isn't measured in immediate royalties. It's measured in:
1. **Algorithmic threshold crossing** — enough streams/saves to trigger Discover Weekly → free streams forever
2. **Follower conversion** — new followers = Release Radar placement on every future release
3. **Playlist placement** — stream velocity + save rate triggers editorial consideration
4. **Compound returns** — a follower gained today earns royalties on every future release

**The real math:** $50 spent → 50 new Spotify followers → those 50 followers stream your next 10 releases at avg 3 streams each → 1,500 additional streams over 12 months → ~$7.50 in royalties + continued algorithmic fuel.

At this stage, paid promotion is an **investment in algorithmic compounding**, not a revenue-positive activity.

## 8. WHAT NOT TO BUY

| Service | Why It's a Waste |
|---------|-----------------|
| Playlist placement services (SubmitHub premium, PlaylistPush, etc.) | Most playlists are bot-inflated. Streams don't convert to followers. Spotify penalizes artificial streams. |
| "Guaranteed streams" packages | Bot farms. Will get your music flagged and potentially removed. |
| PR agencies/retainers at this stage | $2K–5K/month for press that doesn't convert to streams at your level. Revisit post-50K monthly listeners. |
| Blog placements (paid) | Blog readership is dead for music discovery. Save the money. |
| Radio promotion | Requires $5K+ minimum and doesn't serve indie R&B at your scale. |
| Instagram "growth services" | Bot follows, pod engagement, or fake followers. All suppress real reach. |

**The only paid services worth considering (post-Jul 10 if CREAM greenlit):**
- Blossom PR (mission-based, short-burst campaigns around major releases only)
- A freelance Meta Ads specialist ($500–1K one-time to audit and optimize your campaigns if you hit $300+/month spend)

## 9. DISCOVERY MODE DEEP DIVE

Discovery Mode deserves its own section because it's the most powerful tool at your scale — $0 upfront, purely performance-based.

### How the Royalty Math Works

Normal royalty: ~$0.004–$0.005/stream
Discovery Mode royalty: ~$0.003–$0.0035/stream (30% reduction)

**On 1,000 streams:**
- Normal: $4.50
- Discovery Mode: $3.15
- Cost of Discovery Mode: $1.35

**What you get for that $1.35:**
- +50% more saves (proven average)
- +44% more playlist adds
- Accelerated algorithmic placement
- More streams overall (net positive even at reduced rate)

### When Discovery Mode is NET POSITIVE

If Discovery Mode generates even 40% more total streams, the increased volume at reduced rate EXCEEDS what you'd earn without it:

- Without DM: 1,000 streams × $0.0045 = $4.50
- With DM: 1,400 streams × $0.00315 = $4.41

Breakeven is ~40% stream lift. Average lift is 50%+. **Discovery Mode is almost always net positive on tracks that already convert.**

### Decision Tree

```
Track released → Wait 7 days → Check save rate
    → Save rate ≥ 3%? → ACTIVATE Discovery Mode
    → Save rate 2–3%? → Wait 14 more days. If trending up, activate.
    → Save rate < 2%? → DO NOT activate. Track has a product problem, not a distribution problem.
```
