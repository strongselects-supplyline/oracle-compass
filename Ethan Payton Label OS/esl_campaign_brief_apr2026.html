What Makes SF the Bridge Single
Desire + Ache Duality
The bridge single works when both genders can project into it simultaneously. Male listeners hear "she's frustrating me." Female listeners hear "he finally gets what it feels like." The KAYTRANADA-influenced production (guitar riff, warm mid-tempo energy) is the sonic hook that the 40% female audience already in the catalog will recognize as something for them.
