## 3. SYNC LICENSING (Highest ROI per hour invested)

### Why Sync is Priority #1 for Revenue Diversification

| Factor | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Single TV placement | $500–$5,000 |
| National ad placement | $5,000–$50,000 |
| Gaming placement | $1,000–$10,000 |
| Micro-sync (YouTube, TikTok, podcast) | $50–$500 per placement |
| Time to first placement | 2–6 months after submission |
| Ongoing effort after setup | Near zero (catalog works for you) |

A single mid-tier TV sync ($2,000) equals 400K–500K streams at indie royalty rates. That's 20x your current monthly listeners in a single placement. Sync is where the math actually works for indie artists.

### Your Sync Advantages

1. **Catalog depth:** 13 released tracks + 5 EP tracks + 5 vault singles = 23 tracks by August. Supervisors want options.
2. **Mood range:** Sexy (0.73 avg), Chill (0.56 avg), Romantic (Hollywood Fever). Covers multiple licensing needs.
3. **Clean vocals:** No features, no samples to clear. Sync-ready.
4. **Full ownership:** You own masters + publishing. No split-sheet complications for licensing deals.
5. **Instrumental availability:** If you have or can create instrumental versions, that doubles your placement opportunities (many placements need instrumental-only).

### What Sync Supervisors Look For (2026)

| Criteria | Your Status |
|----------|-------------|
| High production quality | ✅ (Illangelo chain mastering) |
| Clean masters (no excessive compression, -14 to -16 LUFS for sync) | ⚠️ Your masters are -6 LUFS (loud). Need alt sync masters at -14 LUFS. |
| Instrumental versions available | ❌ NEEDED — create instrumental bounces for all tracks |
| Stems available on request | ⚠️ You have sessions. Need to export labeled stems (vocals, beat, bass, keys). |
| Mood-tagged and searchable | ❌ NEEDED — create sync one-sheet with mood/tempo/use-case tags |
| Short (2–3 min) AND long (full) versions | ⚠️ Most tracks are standard length. "Sync edits" (60-sec versions) would help. |

### The Sync Setup Checklist (One-Time, ~4 hours total)

- [ ] **Create instrumental versions** for top 10 tracks (bounce from sessions, remove vocal bus)
- [ ] **Bounce sync masters** at -14 LUFS / -1dB True Peak (quieter, more dynamic range than streaming masters)
- [ ] **Export stems** for top 5 most sync-friendly tracks (vocal, instrumental, bass, drums separately)
- [ ] **Write sync one-sheet** — 1-page PDF: artist name, contact, each track with BPM/key/mood/suggested use-case
- [ ] **Register with sync platforms** (see below)

### Sync Platforms to Register With

| Platform | Type | Cost | Best For |
|----------|------|------|----------|
| **Musicbed** | Curated marketplace | Free to submit; they take 50% of placement fee | Film, advertising, high-end brands |
| **Artlist** | Subscription marketplace | Artist program (revenue share) | YouTube creators, corporate video, ads |
| **Songtradr** | Marketplace + AI matching | Free tier available | Volume placements, micro-sync |
| **Music Gateway** | Marketplace + pitching | Free to submit | TV, film, gaming |
| **Epidemic Sound** | Exclusive catalog deals | Requires exclusivity (per-track basis) | Recurring revenue but loss of control |
| **Sync licensing agent (freelance)** | Personal representative | 15–25% commission | High-value placements, relationships |

**Recommended path:**
1. Start with **Songtradr** (free, AI-matched, volume) and **Musicbed** (curated, higher-value placements)
2. Add **Music Gateway** for gaming opportunities (gaming sync is booming — $1.91B market in 2026)
3. Skip Epidemic Sound (exclusivity requirement conflicts with your independent distribution)
4. At 50K+ monthly listeners, consider a freelance sync agent for premium placements

### The Drop Model (Not Always-On)

Don't run an always-open store. Run limited drops tied to releases.

**Why drops work better than a permanent store:**
- Scarcity drives action (24–72 hour windows)
- Drops become content (countdown, reveal, sold-out announcement)
- No stale inventory sitting around
- Aligns with the Mystique Protocol (selective revelation)

**Drop template:**
1. Tease on Close Friends story (48 hrs before)
2. Announce on feed (24 hrs before)
3. Drop goes live (link in bio, 72-hour window or "until sold out")
4. Sold-out/close announcement (creates FOMO for next drop)
5. Ship within 7 days (print-on-demand handles this automatically)

## 8. CATALOG AS ASSET (Long-Term Wealth Building)

### The Real-Estate Analogy

Every song you release is a property that generates rent (royalties) indefinitely. A 23-track catalog streaming at 60K/month is a $3,000/year asset. A 50-track catalog at 200K/month is a $12,000/year asset. A 100-track catalog at 500K/month is a $30,000/year asset.

**Investment firms are buying song catalogs at 10–30x annual revenue.** A catalog earning $12K/year could sell for $120K–$360K.

You're not just making music. You're building an asset portfolio.

### How to Maximize Catalog Value

| Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Release consistently (every 2 weeks during waterfall, monthly minimum after) | More properties = more rent |
| Keep save rates high (3%+) | Properties that retain listeners are worth more |
| Maintain full ownership (masters + publishing) | 100% equity = maximum asset value |
| Keep tracks sync-ready (instrumentals, stems, clean metadata) | Increases licensing potential = higher valuation |
| Don't delete or pull down underperformers | Even low-stream tracks contribute to catalog depth for assessment |
| Register everything properly (ASCAP, MLC, Songtrust, SoundExchange) | Ensures all royalty streams are collected |

### The Registration Checklist (Compliance, post-release)

These happen the Thursday AFTER each release:

- [ ] **ASCAP** — Register new works (songwriter/publisher share)
- [ ] **MLC (Mechanical Licensing Collective)** — Register for mechanical royalties
- [ ] **Songtrust** — Global publishing administration (collects from 60+ countries)
- [ ] **SoundExchange** — Digital performance royalties (non-interactive streams: radio, Pandora)
- [ ] **Amuse metadata** — Verify ISRC, UPC, songwriter credits are correct

**Standing status:** Rights infrastructure is COMPLETE (Distance Over Time pub co, ASCAP writer, Songtrust, SoundExchange, IP lawyer, Passman book). This is a maintenance task, not a build task.

## 9. EMAIL LIST (The Missing Asset)

### Why Email Matters in 2026

You own zero direct audience connection. Every follower lives on a platform you don't control (Spotify, IG, TikTok). If any platform suppresses you, changes its algorithm, or shuts down, you lose access to your audience.

Email is the only channel where:
- You control the distribution (no algorithm between you and the reader)
- Open rates average 25–40% (vs. 5–10% organic reach on IG)
- It's the #1 converter for merch drops and event tickets
- It proves audience depth to potential partners/labels/managers

### Setup (30 min, once)

1. **Platform:** ConvertKit (free up to 1,000 subscribers) or Mailchimp (free up to 500)
2. **Embed on ethanpayton.com** (Wix has native email form widgets)
3. **Incentive:** "Get early access to new music before it hits streaming" or "Join the late-night list — unreleased demos, behind-the-scenes, and first dibs on everything"
4. **Collection points:** IG bio link (via Linktree), smart link pages (post-stream), website popup

### Growth Target

| Timeline | Subscribers | How |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| Jul 2026 | 50 | Soft launch: Close Friends announcement + bio link |
| Oct 2026 | 200 | Active promotion: every release smart link includes email capture |
| Jan 2027 | 500 | Merch drop email-first access drives signups |
| Jul 2027 | 1,000 | Consistent value delivery = organic word-of-mouth |

### Email Content (Monthly max, don't over-send)
