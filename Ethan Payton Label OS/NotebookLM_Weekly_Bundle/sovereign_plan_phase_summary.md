### The Artist Definition

You are not a content creator who makes music. You are a **recording artist who uses content as distribution infrastructure**. You are doing what major labels stopped doing: developing an artist with rigor, intentionality, and a long-game view. Label OS is the machine that replaced A&R.

### The Five Content Pillars

These are not separate content types. They are five doors into the same world. Range within the lane — depth, not width.

1. **The Release** — The music itself. Announcement posts, Canvas loops, snippets. Sells the *transformation*, not the product.
2. **Talk to 'Em** — Face. Direct address. The emotional truth behind the record. Hook in line 1 before the fold.
3. **World-Building** — Milwaukee. The east side. 3AM. The specific geography and emotional weather the music lives inside.
4. **Cinematic Visual** — Canvas loops, B-roll. Deep emerald, gold warmth, noir shadow, grainy 35mm. The consistent visual signature.
5. **Community Trace** — Comments on T4 artist pages. Gorilla Geo DMs. Reaction clips. Lane 1 before Lane 2.

### The Portfolio Test

Before every post: **"Would this attract the collaborators, playlist curators, and listeners I want in Year 2?"** Post what you want to be discovered as.

### The Caption Doctrine

- **Line 1 must stop the scroll** before the "more" cutoff.
- Deep philosophical voice lives in lines 2+.
- SEE ME post ("I KNOW YOU HEAR ME, BUT DO YOU SEE ME?") executed this correctly.
