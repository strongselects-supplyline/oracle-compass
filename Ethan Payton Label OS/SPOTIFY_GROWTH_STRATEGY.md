## IV. ENGAGEMENT CONVERSION STRATEGY

### The Core Problem
76% programmed streams + 1.2% save rate + 4% monthly active = **high reach, low return**. The algorithm delivers listeners who don't convert. The HF audience mismatch (documented in V3 diagnosis) means the algorithmically-delivered cohort expects warm/chill/lo-fi and gets dark/foreground/TrapSoul.

### The Fix (3-pronged)
1. **Cohort correction via EP:** The EP leads with Cluster A material (GL, SF, WU2) to attract the dark-R&B curator cohort. If EP save rates exceed HF-era norms (>2%), the algorithm is learning a new model of who Ethan's listener is.

2. **CTA discipline:** Every content piece, every social post, every story = "Save [track name] on Spotify." Not "stream" (streams don't compound into algorithmic weight the way saves do). Not "link in bio" (too passive). The specific track name matters because it routes the save to the right algorithmic bucket.

3. **Core Drive tiered engagement:** Per the 5-tier matrix:
   - T0 (SZA, Weeknd, Drake) → Meta ad audiences ONLY. Too big for DM.
   - T1 (Faiyaz, Majid Jordan, Tiller, PND) → Follow + study content cadence + use as editorial pitch comps
   - T2 (Xavier Omär, Sabrina Claudio, bLAck pARty, 6LACK) → Genuine comments 2-3x/week during sprint
   - T3 (Arin Ray, Eli Sostre, Topaz Jones, Destin Conrad, Mairi, Che Ecru) → DM within 30 days of release
   - T4 (KAYTRANADA, Anderson .Paak, Vinylz, Nineteen85) → Follow, monitor credits
