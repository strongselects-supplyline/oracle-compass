Spotify paid $11B to the music industry in 2025 — largest payout from any retailer ever. 13,800 artists now earn $100K+/year from Spotify alone. The user-centric payout model (your subscription fee goes to artists YOU listen to, not the global pool) showed 14% indie artist earnings growth in France/Germany rollouts. If this goes global, it directly benefits artists like you with dedicated listeners.

Streaming isn't growing at 20% anymore — the easy adoption markets are saturated. What's left is slower growth and increased competition for attention. Save rate → Discover Weekly → algorithm is still the lever, but the bar is rising.

TRAP 2: Context-switching tax

Dash → studio → content → admin in one day. Every switch costs 20 min of ramp-up your ADHD brain can't afford.

COUNTERMOVE: Single-mode days. One primary, one secondary. Never three.

A track at 85% that's OUT beats a track at 100% in your vault. You have 40 tracks. Ship them. The catalog compounds — perfection doesn't.
