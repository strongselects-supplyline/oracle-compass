# The Operator's Toolchain & Deliverables Engine

Because you are acting as the Visual Director, Creator, Editor, and Publisher simultaneously, your toolchain cannot have overlap. Every software must execute a specific function in the flywheel, outputting a precise deliverable. 

Here is the audited stack separating what you **already have**, the **distributor plugins** you must use, the **growth engines** you should add, and the **universal checklist** it all feeds into.

---

## 1. The Core Distribution Matrix (The Hub)
Your central nervous system for rights, royalties, and structural delivery.

| Tool | Actionable Deliverable | Jutsu (Workflow Rule) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Amuse / DistroKid** | ISRC codes, audio distribution to DSPs | **The 3-Week Rule:** Upload the `.wav` 21 days prior to release to guarantee native Spotify editorial pitching time. |
| **Musixmatch Pro** | Verified lyrics synced to Spotify/Apple/IG | **The Backdoor:** Verify for free *through* your distributor's partner portal to secure the Spotify for Artists checkmark seamlessly. |
| **Masterchannel** | Final AI-driven mastering | **The Final Polish:** If the mix isn't perfectly glued, run it through here ($5.99) inside Amuse before pressing "Submit". |

## 2. Creative & Visual Production (What You Own)
Mapping your existing **Adobe Suite**, **GoDaddy Studio Pro**, and **CapCut** to their definitive roles so you don't stall choosing what app to open.

| Tool | Actionable Deliverable | Jutsu (Workflow Rule) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **GoDaddy Studio Pro** | Primary Cover Art (Mobile) | **The On-the-Go Drop:** Use the DALL-E prompts generated by Label OS to instantly composite/typography your covers on your phone. |
| **Photoshop** | YT Thumbnails, IG Grid Assets, Cover Art touchups | **The Hierarchy:** Design the 3000x3000px primary art first, then use crop logic to scale down to 16:9 for YouTube and 4:5 for Instagram. |
| **After Effects** | Spotify Canvas, Spotify Visualizers | **The Canvas Loop:** Export purely 720x1280 8-second MP4s (max 8MB) to hit Spotify's exact seamless looping specs. |
| **Premiere Pro** | Hero Music Video (A-Tier content) | **The Anchor:** Exclusively use this for long-form narrative. Everything else gets pushed to CapCut to avoid timeline bloat. |
| **Lightroom** | Artist Press Photos, Hero Shots | **The Palette Pull:** Batch color-grade your baseline photos to match the deep emerald/gold brand voice parameters so your visual identity is locked. |
| **CapCut** | Teasers, TikToks, IG Reels | **The Fragment:** Take the Premiere Pro hero video or studio footage and chop it into 5-10 second hyper-edits synced strictly to the song's hook. |

## 3. The Multiplier Engine (What You Are Missing)
As a one-man publisher, you cannot do manual PR/Ad execution all day. You need to plug in these scaling tools to automate the "Publisher" side of the flywheel.

| Publisher Tool | Actionable Deliverable | Jutsu (Workflow Rule) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **OpusClip** | 10-20 auto-generated Reels/Shorts | **The Content Factory:** Feed OpusClip your Premiere hero video. It uses AI to auto-cut 15 viral shorts. You export them to CapCut for final polish. You secure 2 weeks of social posting from zero manual editing. |
| **SongTools** | Playlist Pitching (150k+ lists), Auto IG/TikTok Ads | **The Algorithm Primer:** Deploy $50-$100 on a SongTools campaign on release day to drive immediate localized traffic, forcing Spotify's "Release Radar" to trigger. |
| **Groover** | Curator & Influencer pitches | **The Micro-Pitch:** Use the Copy Vault in Label OS to generate your 1-liner pitch, and blast it to 20 curators 7 days before release for guaranteed written feedback/playlist adds. |
| **un:hurd** | Data heatmaps & Audience Intel | **The Echo:** Use un:hurd to track exactly *who* is saving the track, then feed that exact demographic data back into the PR Copy Vault for the next release (Z into A). |

## 📋 The Universal Per-Release Deliverables Checklist
Every time you initiate a new project in the Label OS, the ultimate goal is to churn out these exact files.

**Pre-Release (Weeks -3 to -1):**
- [ ] `track_master.wav` (DAW or Masterchannel)
- [ ] `cover_art.jpg` (3000x3000 | GoDaddy / Photoshop)
- [ ] `lyrics.txt` (Label OS Vault → Musixmatch)
- [ ] `pitch_copy.txt` (Label OS Copy Dept → Spotify for Artists / Groover)
- [ ] `hero_video.mp4` (Premiere Pro)

**Release Day (Week 0):**
- [ ] `spotify_canvas.mp4` (Vertical | 8s Loop | After Effects)
- [ ] `youtube_visualizer.mp4` (Horizontal | After Effects)
- [ ] `announcement_graphic.png` (Square/Portrait | GoDaddy / Photoshop)
- [ ] `launch_ad_creative.mp4` (Vertical | CapCut → Fed to SongTools)

**Post-Release Engine (Weeks +1 to +4):**
- [ ] `15x_shortform_clips.mp4` (OpusClip → CapCut → Daily upload queue)
- [ ] `campaign_intel.csv` (SongTools / un:hurd data → Fed back into Label OS Vault)
